About

I am a Graduate student at University of Minnesota - Twin Cities pursuing a Master's Program in Robotics Engineering expected to graduate in June 2025. My interests lie in the intersection of Perception, SLAM, Path Planning, and integrating Machine Learning into Robotics. I am currently Working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Minnesota Robotics Institute advised by Prof. Ce Yang. I have two years of professional work experience as a Software Developer at Tata Consultancy Services where I got the opportunity to develop robust backend systems for multiple international clients including BestBuy. I have worked extensively on System Design, Development and Integration for handling and processing well over 25000 daily requests.

Through my Master's, I have focussed majority of my coursework in the areas of Core Robotics concepts, Machine Learning and NLP. My current course's include Intelligent Robotic Systems, Optimal Control and Reinforcement Learning

I previously worked as a Graduate Research Assistant at LTI, School of Computer Science. I was advised by Prof. Bhiksha Raj. My research was focussed in Learning strategies for unsupervised adaptations of test data to bridge the distribution gap in test and train data so as to improve classification accuracy.
